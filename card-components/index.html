Card Components

Wrap Media

A Wrap is essentially a mobile app that lives at a URL.

Wraps are made up cards— a new "page" of the app is a card. The way that myself and the design team designed wraps was by designing cards.

Wrap Components are the building blocks of those cards. They can be positioned at will on the card.

Custom Component — Client

Share and E-commerce Interactions for Tegan & Sara's Merchandise Catalog

Wrap's Studio Design Team and Warner Bros. Records partnered to create a mobile-first catalog for merch surrounding Tegan & Sara's "Love You 2 Death" Tour.

Working closely with the coolest visual designer around, Theo Arguna, I designed and implemented the "Commerce" and "Share" interactions.

See the Pen Cart & Share Components by eric smith (@erchsm) on CodePen.

Custom Component — In Product

"Find Nearby" Component for the Small Business

As one of our "in-product" components we built an integration with the Google Places API's for helping users find the nearest location of that service near them. For the case below the customer was a small gym business and a user could find a branch local to their location.

This component was a "self serve" part of our product. Anyone with an account could add this to a card. I worked on the motion and interaction here and I worked directly with the Google API's while developing this.

See the Pen Nearby Services Component by eric smith (@erchsm) on CodePen.
