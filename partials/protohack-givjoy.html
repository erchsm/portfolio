What we made— GivJoy

Givjoy is a web and mobile application for allowing a person to bring joy to their friends. GivJoy does this by creating a marketplace wherein a joy-sender can employ a person with a joy-giving, creative talents to bring joy to their friends. GivJoy was created at ProtoHack, a code-free hackathon, in 9 hours.

Our ProtoHack team consisted of 5 individuals–3 Buisiness Development experts and 1 other UX Designer besides myself. I was by far the most junior person in the team–Most of the others had been in their respective industries for 5+ years. I wanted to pull my own weight in the team and prove myself.

This prototype was made with Principle and Sketch.
